  • Product Information
    If you can dream it, you can build it with the 'Minecraft' Xbox 360 Edition game. This fantasy game allows gamers to create worlds using their imaginations. Play as a single player or let friends join in on the building fun. With Xbox LIVE, up to eight people can play together. In the game, players must create a shelter for themselves, which is used for protection after night falls, which is when the scary monsters come out. It does not stop there though. In Minecraft, players craft everything you can think of, from pets to castles. The sky is the limit.
